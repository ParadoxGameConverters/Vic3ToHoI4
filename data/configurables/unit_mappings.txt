#  45 infantry
#  10 motorized
#  15 artillery brigades
#   5 support artillery
#  10 light armour

#  1 armored division    (6 LA, 3 MOT, 1 SA)
#  1 mechanized division (3 LA, 6 MOT, 1 SA)
#  5 infantry divisions  (45 INF, 15 ART)
